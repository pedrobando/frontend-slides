Part 1

The 9 Concepts, In Plain Language

Each one: what it is, our buyer Marisol & Jay (first-time buyers near Orlando), and one action.

Concept 01
The 3-Campaign Map
What it is
Three separate campaigns: Cold (strangers), Warm (engaged), Retarget (almost). Each excludes the one before it.
Tres campañas separadas: Cold (desconocidos), Warm (interactuaron), Retarget (casi). Cada una excluye a la anterior.
Marisol & Jay
First time, strangers in Orlando. Second time, watchers. Third time, almosts.
Primera vez, desconocidos en Orlando. Segunda, los que vieron. Tercera, los casi.
Do this
Build three campaigns, not one with three ad sets. Otherwise the same person sees all three and you pay three times.
Construye tres campañas, no una con tres ad sets. Si no, la misma persona ve las tres y pagas tres veces.
Concept 02
Campaign, Ad Set, Ad (three floors)
What it is
Campaign = the building (your goal). Ad set = the floor (who, budget, where). Ad = the room (the video and words).
Campaña = el edificio (tu meta). Ad set = el piso (quién, presupuesto, dónde). Ad = el cuarto (el video y las palabras).
Marisol & Jay
Goal: conversations. Floor: near Orlando, $20/day. Room: the reel that says "I help first-time buyers stop renting."
Meta: conversaciones. Piso: cerca de Orlando, $20/día. Cuarto: el reel que dice "ayudo a primerizos a dejar de alquilar."
Do this
Before you click, say which floor you are on.
Antes de hacer clic, di en qué piso estás.
Concept 03
Objective
What it is
Tells Meta what human to find. Engagement finds commenters, Leads finds form-fillers, Conversions finds bookers.
Le dice a Meta qué humano buscar. Engagement busca los que comentan, Leads los que llenan formularios, Conversions los que agendan.
Marisol & Jay
Cold = Engagement, Warm = Leads, Retarget = Conversions.
Cold = Engagement, Warm = Leads, Retarget = Conversions.
Do this
Match the objective to the next action you want, not the one you wish for.
Empareja el objetivo con la próxima acción que quieres, no la que deseas.
Concept 04
Special Ad Category: Housing
What it is
Real estate ads are declared Housing. Meta locks age, gender, tight location. The copy does the targeting instead.
Los anuncios de bienes raíces se declaran Housing. Meta bloquea edad, género, ubicación apretada. El copy hace el targeting.
Marisol & Jay
"First-time buyers near Orlando, tired of renting" filters better than any age slider.
"Compradores primerizos cerca de Orlando, cansados de alquilar" filtra mejor que cualquier slider de edad.
Do this
Declare Housing on step one, every time. (Recruiting agents is the exception: Employment.)
Declara Housing en el paso uno, siempre. (Reclutar agentes es la excepción: Employment.)
Concept 05
Where the lead lands
What it is
When someone taps, they land in a message (Messenger, Instagram, WhatsApp). A small auto-greeting starts the conversation.
Cuando alguien toca, aterriza en un mensaje (Messenger, Instagram, WhatsApp). Un saludito automático arranca la conversación.
Marisol & Jay
She taps at midnight, a message asks "are you renting right now?" and she answers.
Toca a medianoche, un mensaje pregunta "¿estás alquilando ahora?" y ella responde.
Do this
Wire the greeting so nobody who taps gets silence.
Monta el saludo para que nadie que toque reciba silencio.
Concept 06
Audience + the exclusion cascade
What it is
Two levers survive Housing: a location, and saved lists of people by what they did. Cold excludes warm, warm excludes leads, retarget excludes bookers.
Dos palancas sobreviven a Housing: una ubicación, y listas guardadas de gente por lo que hicieron. Cold excluye warm, warm excluye leads, retarget excluye los que agendaron.
Marisol & Jay
Each campaign excludes the stage before it, so your money hits fresh eyes.
Cada campaña excluye la etapa anterior, así tu dinero llega a ojos frescos.
Do this
Build your lists first, before any ad set (Part 3 below).
Construye tus listas primero, antes de cualquier ad set (Parte 3 abajo).
Concept 07
Budget
What it is
$20 to $50 a day per campaign. Small enough that a bad week does not hurt, big enough for Meta to learn. Plan to spend about 10% of what you want to earn.
$20 a $50 al día por campaña. Suficiente chico para que una mala semana no duela, suficiente grande para que Meta aprenda. Planifica gastar como 10% de lo que quieres ganar.
Marisol & Jay
$20/day on cold for two weeks is $280 to find out if your message lands. Cheap tuition.
$20/día en cold por dos semanas son $280 para saber si tu mensaje pega. Matrícula barata.
Do this
Pick a daily number you will not flinch at, then leave it alone to learn.
Escoge un número diario que no te haga temblar, después déjalo quieto para que aprenda.
Concept 08
Placement
What it is
Where your ad shows (reels, feed, stories). Advantage+ placements lets Meta choose, right for most of you. Manual is hand-picking, only with data.
Dónde aparece tu anuncio (reels, feed, stories). Advantage+ placements deja que Meta escoja, correcto para casi todos. Manual es a mano, solo con datos.
Marisol & Jay
Your vertical reel pours naturally into reels and stories where she scrolls at night.
Tu reel vertical se vuelca naturalmente en reels y stories donde ella hace scroll de noche.
Do this
Leave it on Advantage+ until you have a reason on paper to change it.
Déjalo en Advantage+ hasta que tengas una razón en papel para cambiarlo.
Concept 09
The metrics that matter (your scoreboard)
What it is
Ignore likes. Watch cost per result, click rate (CTR), cost per 1,000 (CPM), frequency, and results. Your headline number: cost per conversation.
Ignora los likes. Mira costo por resultado, tasa de clics (CTR), costo por 1,000 (CPM), frecuencia, y resultados. Tu número principal: costo por conversación.
Marisol & Jay
A cheap lead who never replies is not cheap. An expensive lead who becomes a client is a bargain.
Un lead barato que nunca responde no es barato. Un lead caro que se vuelve cliente es una ganga.
Do this
After today, read only these numbers and write down your cost per conversation.
Después de hoy, lee solo estos números y anota tu costo por conversación.
Part 2

Build The Cold Campaign, Click By Click

These screens match Pedro's real Ads Manager exactly. Go top to bottom. Labels in blue are the exact words you will see on screen.

1Create the campaign + pick the objective
From the Campaigns dashboard, click Create. In Create new campaign, leave buying type on Auction. Under Choose a campaign objective, select Engagement. Click Continue.
Desde el panel de Campaigns, haz clic en Create. En Create new campaign, deja el buying type en Auction. Bajo Choose a campaign objective, escoge Engagement. Haz clic en Continue.
2Choose the campaign setup
On Choose a campaign setup, pick Tailored messages campaign. Click Continue.
En Choose a campaign setup, escoge Tailored messages campaign. Haz clic en Continue.
3Name it + set the budget strategy
At the campaign level, set Campaign name to 01 Cold [your name] AIDA. Under Budget strategy, choose Ad set budget. Leave Campaign bid strategy on Highest volume.
En el nivel de campaña, pon Campaign name como 01 Cold [tu nombre] AIDA. Bajo Budget strategy, escoge Ad set budget. Deja Campaign bid strategy en Highest volume.
4Declare Special Ad Category: Housing
Scroll to Special Ad Categories. Open Categories and choose Housing. Set Countries to United States. Click Next. Do this now, you cannot add it after publishing without rebuilding.
Baja a Special Ad Categories. Abre Categories y escoge Housing. Pon Countries en United States. Haz clic en Next. Hazlo ahora, no lo puedes añadir después de publicar sin reconstruir.
This is concept 3 live
From here, age, gender, and tight location are locked. That is expected. Your copy does the targeting.
Desde aquí, edad, género, y ubicación apretada quedan bloqueados. Es lo esperado. Tu copy hace el targeting.
5Ad set: conversion location + page
At the ad set level, under Conversion, set Conversion location to Message destinations. Select your Facebook Page.
En el nivel de ad set, bajo Conversion, pon Conversion location en Message destinations. Selecciona tu Facebook Page.
6Pick your messaging apps
Choose Manual destination and check the apps you actually answer: Messenger, Instagram, WhatsApp. This is concept 4, where the lead lands.
Escoge Manual destination y marca las apps que de verdad contestas: Messenger, Instagram, WhatsApp. Este es el concepto 4, dónde aterriza el lead.
7Performance goal
Set Performance goal to Maximize number of conversations. That tells Meta to find people who actually message.
Pon Performance goal en Maximize number of conversations. Eso le dice a Meta que busque gente que de verdad manda mensajes.
8Budget & schedule
Under Budget & schedule, set Daily budget to your number ($20 to $50). Set a start date. Leave the end date off so it keeps running. This is concept 7.
Bajo Budget & schedule, pon Daily budget en tu número ($20 a $50). Pon fecha de inicio. Deja la fecha de fin apagada para que siga corriendo. Este es el concepto 7.
9Location (your one real targeting lever)
Under Audience controls → Locations, set your city plus a radius, like Orlando, Florida + 15mi. This is concept 6.
Bajo Audience controls → Locations, pon tu ciudad más un radio, como Orlando, Florida + 15mi. Este es el concepto 6.
10Placements + advertiser
Under Placements, leave Advantage+ on (let Meta choose). Under Ad transparency, set the advertiser to your verified business. This is concept 8.
Bajo Placements, deja Advantage+ encendido (deja que Meta escoja). Bajo Ad transparency, pon el anunciante como tu negocio verificado. Este es el concepto 8.
You will see this and it is fine
In Detailed targeting, Age is forced to 18 to 65+ and Gender to All. That is Housing locking it. Do not fight it.
En Detailed targeting, Age se fuerza a 18 a 65+ y Gender a All. Eso es Housing bloqueándolo. No pelees con eso.
11Ad level: identity
At the ad level, name the ad. Under Identity, confirm your Facebook Page and Instagram profile. Under Destination, choose Messaging apps and check the same apps as step 6.
En el nivel de ad, nombra el anuncio. Bajo Identity, confirma tu Facebook Page e Instagram profile. Bajo Destination, escoge Messaging apps y marca las mismas apps del paso 6.
12Ad creative + the conversation + publish
Under Ad creative, add your video (a video is required). Under Conversations, set the greeting and the quick questions people see after they tap. Check your Tracking events. Then click the green Publish.
Bajo Ad creative, añade tu video (un video es requerido). Bajo Conversations, pon el saludo y las preguntas rápidas que la gente ve después de tocar. Revisa tus eventos de Tracking. Después haz clic en el Publish verde.
No video yet?
Build everything else and leave it in draft. The campaign waits for you. A campaign with no video has nothing to say.
¿No tienes video todavía? Construye todo lo demás y déjalo en borrador. La campaña te espera. Una campaña sin video no tiene nada que decir.
Part 3

Build Your Audiences (the cascade)

This is what you built in Session 6. Ads Manager menu → Audiences. These lists power the warm and retarget campaigns.

AStart a custom audience
On the Audiences page, click Create audience → Custom audience. Click Next.
En la página de Audiences, haz clic en Create audience → Custom audience. Haz clic en Next.
BWarm list: Facebook Page engagement
Choose source Facebook Page. For Events, pick People who engaged with your Page. Retention 90 days. Name it Warm-Engagers-90d. Click Create audience.
Escoge la fuente Facebook Page. Para Events, escoge People who engaged with your Page. Retención 90 días. Nombra Warm-Engagers-90d. Haz clic en Create audience.
CRetarget list: video viewers
New custom audience, source Video. Under Choose an engagement type, pick the heavy watchers: People who either completed or viewed at least 15 seconds (ThruPlay) or at least 50% / 75%. Name it Retarget-Viewers.
Nueva custom audience, fuente Video. Bajo Choose an engagement type, escoge los que ven mucho: People who either completed or viewed at least 15 seconds (ThruPlay) o at least 50% / 75%. Nombra Retarget-Viewers.
DAdd Instagram engagement
One more custom audience, source Instagram profile. For Events, pick People who engaged with this professional account. Add this into your warm list so Instagram and Facebook engagers are both covered.
Una custom audience más, fuente Instagram profile. Para Events, escoge People who engaged with this professional account. Añade esto a tu lista warm para cubrir engagers de Instagram y Facebook.
The cascade, in plain terms
Cold runs to your location and quietly fills Warm-Engagers-90d. Warm talks to that list and excludes anyone who became a lead. Retarget talks to Retarget-Viewers and excludes anyone who booked. Build the lists, then attach them as Include or Exclude inside each ad set.
Cold corre a tu ubicación y calladito llena Warm-Engagers-90d. Warm le habla a esa lista y excluye a quien se hizo lead. Retarget le habla a Retarget-Viewers y excluye a quien agendó. Construye las listas, después adjuéntalas como Include o Exclude dentro de cada ad set.

Scoreboard

What To Measure From Now On

Your one number
Cost per conversation = ad spend divided by real conversations started. Check it weekly. A cheap lead who never replies is not cheap. An expensive lead who becomes a client is a bargain. Also glance at click rate (are they tapping?) and frequency (am I showing it too much?).
Costo por conversación = gasto del anuncio dividido por conversaciones reales iniciadas. Revísalo cada semana. Un lead barato que nunca responde no es barato. Un lead caro que se vuelve cliente es una ganga. También mira la tasa de clics (¿están tocando?) y la frecuencia (¿lo estoy mostrando demasiado?).
