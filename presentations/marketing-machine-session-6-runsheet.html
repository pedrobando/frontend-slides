Building the 3-Campaign Meta Ads System — Live

Pedro's recruiting demo · Special Ad Category: Employment · Milwaukee + Orlando. Agents replicate for their Housing (buyer/seller) campaigns — same clicks, different category.

How to use this sheet Each step has two columns: DO = the exact click in Ads Manager / Canva / ManyChat. SAY = the line you say out loud while you do it. Go top to bottom. The AI-copy step comes before each campaign so the room writes copy with you. Build order never changes: Audiences → Cold → Warm → Retarget → Follow-up → Publish.

Step 0

Build Your Audiences First

Always before the ad sets. Saves ~5 min per campaign and is what makes the exclusion cascade possible. Ads Manager → Audiences → Create Custom Audience.

Audience A — Warm-Engagers-90d
A
Do

Source: IG account · engaged + FB Page · engaged + Video · watched 75%+ of cold reel. Retention: 90 days. Name it Warm-Engagers-90d. Save.

Say

"This is everyone who's touched my content in the last 90 days. Cold builds this list for me for free — then warm talks only to them."

Audience B — Retarget custom audience
B
Do

Source: 50–75% video viewers of the warm video + Calendly visitors who didn't book. Retention 90 days. Name Retarget-Warm-NoBook.

Say

"These people raised their hand and then ghosted. Tiny list, hottest leads. We'll hit them last."

The exclusion cascade (say it out loud) Cold excludes Warm-Engagers-90d → Warm excludes anyone who booked → Retarget excludes booked + pipeline + unfollowers. "That math is the whole game — without it the same person sees three ads and I pay three times."

① Write the copy live (AIDA)

Paste your avatar, run it, read the output to the room. "The framework does the lifting — my only job is making the avatar specific."

Prompt 01 / 05 · paste into Claude / ChatGPTAIDA · Cold
You are a Meta ads copywriter for real estate. Write me a 30-second AIDA
hook for a cold-audience Instagram reel.

==CLIENT AVATAR== (REQUIRED)
[PASTE AVATAR — who they are, where, what they want, what they fear]
(Pedro's: real estate agents 23–35, $1M–$3M volume, 2–4 yrs licensed,
Milwaukee or Central FL, bilingual; pain: "no training, broker just takes splits")

==CAMPAIGN CONTEXT==
Funnel stage: cold (Special Ad Category — Employment)
Format: 30-sec vertical talking-head reel
CTA mechanic: comment-to-DM via ManyChat (keyword: SYSTEMS)
My differentiator: [ONE SENTENCE — live weekly training, real broker, bilingual]

==OUTPUT==
Four beats with timestamps:
A · ATTENTION (0–3s): call out who they are + where they are
I · INTEREST (3–10s): one true thing they didn't know
D · DESIRE (10–20s): name the outcome they want
A · ACTION (20–30s): "comment SYSTEMS and I'll send you X"

Rules: second person, no jargon, no "DM me for info," every line specific.
Then write a Puerto Rican Spanish version with sazón.
Worked example to read aloud A: "Milwaukee or Orlando agent closing 4–12 deals a year — this is for you." · I: "You're not broken, you're building on the wrong foundation." · D: "Live training every Tuesday. Four systems. Bilingual. Two markets." · A: "Comment SYSTEMS for the 48-week curriculum."

==MY DIFFERENTIATOR== [ONE SENTENCE: why me, not another broker]

Prompt 04 / 05IDCA · Retarget
You are a real estate Meta ads copywriter. Write a 15-second IDCA
retargeting script.

==CLIENT AVATAR== (REQUIRED) [PASTE]
==WHAT THEY ALREADY SAW== (REQUIRED) [one line: the cold AIDA + warm
PAS/FAB hook + offer]

==MY CTA OPTIONS== Primary: link in bio (Calendly). Secondary: comment NOW.

==OUTPUT== 4 beats, 15 sec total:
I · IDENTIFY (0–3s): name them by behavior ("you watched X, didn't Y")
D · DISRUPT (3–8s): break the assumption
C · COMPEL (8–12s): real reason to act now (loss-frame works)
A · ACT (12–15s): dual CTA — link in bio AND comment NOW
Direct-to-camera tone. Then PR Spanish w/ sazón.

Follow-UpSCIPAB

Wire the Conversation Layer

The ad is the front door — the money is in the follow-up. 3 touches: 5-min text → 24-hr email → 7-day check-in. All six SCIPAB beats in each.

Prompt 05 / 05SCIPAB · Follow-Up Sequence
You are a real estate follow-up copywriter. Write the SCIPAB sequence
for a lead that just came through Meta ads.

==CLIENT AVATAR== (REQUIRED) [PASTE]
==WHAT THEY RESPONDED TO== (REQUIRED) [e.g. "commented SYSTEMS on a
Tuesday training reel"]
==WHAT I'M OFFERING== [e.g. "48-week training curriculum PDF"]
==ONE-QUESTION FILTER== [segmenting Q, e.g. "where are you producing?"]

==OUTPUT== 3 messages, SCIPAB beats embedded:
MSG 1 (5-min text): S name where they are · C the friction · I cost of
   not fixing · P you as the solver · A reply with one thing · B what they get
MSG 2 (24-hr email, no reply): same structure, new angle, subject line
MSG 3 (7-day text, no reply): shorter, lower pressure, last touch
Rules: under 90 words each, conversational, no "circling back."
Then PR Spanish w/ sazón.
✓
Do

Confirm the 3 ManyChat keyword flows respond: SYSTEMS, COMPARE, NOW. Paste each SCIPAB message into your CRM as a scheduled template (5-min / 24-hr / 7-day).

Say

"Speed is the single biggest lever. The agents who win at Meta aren't the ones with the cheapest leads — they're the ones with the fastest, most human follow-up."

Verify

Master Pre-Publish Checklist

  • SAC declared (Employment) on all 3 campaigns
  • 3 separate campaigns — not 3 ad sets in one
  • Named: 01 Cold / 02 Warm / 03 Retarget
  • Objectives: Engagement / Leads / Conversions
  • Each creative uses its matching framework
  • Cold caption keyword = ManyChat trigger (exact)
  • ManyChat SYSTEMS / COMPARE / NOW all tested
  • Exclusion cascade verified end to end
  • Calendly links are YOUR links
  • Retarget audience 500–2,000 + freq cap 2/wk
  • Budgets set (Cold $20–50 · Warm $100 · Retarget $20–50)
  • SCIPAB 3-touch loaded in CRM
What numbers actually matter (ignore likes) Cost per lead · Lead quality · Speed to lead · Response rate · Appointment rate · Conversion rate. "A cheap lead that never responds isn't cheap. An expensive lead that becomes a client is very profitable."
If It Breaks Live

Recovery Lines — Keep the Demo Moving

Meta blocks the campaign / SAC warning mid-build
Fix: you forgot to declare the category, or changed it after publish. You can't edit SAC after publish — duplicate the campaign, declare Employment first, rebuild. Say: "This is exactly why we declare it on step one."
ManyChat doesn't fire on the keyword
Fix: keyword in the flow must match the caption exactly (case-insensitive, no extra spaces). Re-test in ManyChat preview before moving on. Keep the flow tab open.
Custom audience shows "too small / below 500–1,000"
Fix: expected if cold hasn't run yet. Build the campaign in Draft, don't publish. Say: "Build it now, publish once your cold audience matures — the structure is done either way."
Detailed targeting / age / ZIP options are greyed out
Fix: not a bug — that's SAC working. Say: "Locked on purpose. The creative does the targeting." Move on.
Carousel cards upload out of order
Fix: remove all, re-add card01…card05 one at a time. Order = story order; agitate (card 3) must sit in the middle.
An agent falls behind
Fix: pause at the end of the current campaign, not mid-ad-set. Say: "Catch me up — nobody gets left on a step." Drafts save automatically.
