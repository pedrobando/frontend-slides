The Only Framework You Need

Kill, Scale, or Refresh

Every week. Check the numbers. Make one call.

Kill It
When: Cost/conv > $30 after 14 days AND CTR < 0.5%
Pause. New video + new copy. Try again.
Scale It
When: Cost/conv < $15 AND frequency < 2 after 14 days
Increase budget 20%/week. Never double overnight.
Refresh It
When: Frequency > 3 AND cost/conv climbing week-over-week
Keep campaign. Swap video. New hook, same buyer.
Tape this to your monitor. Every Tuesday: check numbers, make one call. That is the whole system.

Your Tuesday Ritual

5 Minutes. 5 Numbers. Every Week.

Before every meeting, open Ads Manager and answer these five questions.

1
Open Ads Manager
Go to your campaigns tab
2
Cost / Conversation
Going up, down, or sideways?
3
Frequency
Below 2 = good. Above 3 = refresh time.
4
Click Rate (CTR)
Above 1% = healthy. Below 0.5% = message problem.
5
Make the Call
Kill, scale, or refresh. Then come tell me the number.
The agents who fail are not the ones who run bad ads. They are the ones who never look at the numbers.
