Module 04 · The Ask

Surface the Second Person

The Ask Script
Jennifer, one thing that makes a home search smoother is having both decision-makers on the same email and alert system. That way you do not have to forward listings back and forth or worry about missing a showing because one of you saw it first. Want me to add your spouse or partner so you both see what's coming through?
El Script del Ask
Jennifer, una cosa que hace más fácil la búsqueda de casa es tener a las dos personas que deciden en el mismo sistema de alertas. Así no tienes que andar reenviando listings o preocuparte por perderte una visita porque uno lo vio primero. ¿Quieres que añada a tu esposo o pareja para que ambos vean lo que sale?
Framed as convenience for them — not a data grab for you

Module 04 · If They Resist

The Soft Re-Ask

Soft Re-Ask
Totally up to you. Just so you know, our team also offers a financing audit and a market analysis that some clients like to share with their spouse before they commit. If that becomes useful, just send their email when you're ready. No pressure.
El Soft Re-Ask
Sin problema, tú decides. Solo para que sepas, nuestro equipo también ofrece un análisis de financiamiento y un análisis de mercado que algunos clientes les gusta compartir con su pareja antes de comprometerse. Si en algún momento te sirve, me mandas su email cuando estés listo. Sin presión.

Never push. Plant a seed and move on. The soft re-ask converts 30-40% over 90 days.

Module 04 · What Changes

Pitch Both, Not Just One

Once you capture the hidden DM, everything changes.

Email Subjects
Include both names. "Hi Jennifer and Carlos" — never just one.
Text Messages
Send to both numbers. One replies, the other still sees the thread.
Showings
Invite both. The one who shows up alone cannot close.
Lender Intros
Connect both. Pre-approval needs both incomes and credit profiles.

If you only pitch one person, you leave the deal vulnerable to the invisible veto.
