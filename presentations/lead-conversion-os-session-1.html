Module 01 · The Psychology Shift

The Default Move (Why It Fails)

What every agent says
"Hi Maria, this is [Agent] at [Brokerage]. You submitted your info on my website, so I thought I'd give you a quick call and see if you have any questions."

What happens in Maria's brain:

1
She knows you are in sales
2
She knows the only beneficiary of this call is you
3
She defaults to her cheapest defense: "I'm just looking"

That is not a Maria problem. That is a script problem.

Module 01 · Why Real Estate Is Different

The Wall in Their Head

Tow trucks, LASIK surgeons, and emergency plumbers start every conversation with built-in authority. Real estate does not have that gift.

01
"Agents are overpaid."
02
"I can see the photos and price online. I have all the information."
03
"If I ignore this agent, nothing bad happens to me."

Our job: dismantle the wall by establishing authority in the opening seconds and loading the conversation with specific, useful information they cannot get on Zillow.

Module 02 · The 5-Pillar Frame

Your Opening for Every Contact

Callback, voicemail, text, email, open house. No exceptions.

1
Here's Who I Am
Name, brokerage, and the fact you already met through their site.
2
Here's Why I'm Calling
A specific, benefit-led reason. Never "just checking in."
3
I Know What You Want
Mention the keyword, neighborhood, price range, or feature they searched.
4
I Can Help You Get It
Reference our process, a current client, or a reThought service.
5
Here's Something Only I Can Give
A market report. A brief. A relocation package. Something Zillow can't provide.
Pillar 5 is non-negotiable — No specific offer = sales pressure script

Voicemail alone is too thin to act on. Email alone is too easy to ignore. Together they say: this person is organized, specific, and sent me something useful.

Module 02 · What Is Forbidden

Banned Phrases — Effective Today

Never Say ThisReplace With
"Just checking in" "I just walked three properties in your area and the spread is wider than search results show."
"See if you have any questions" "I put together a one-page brief on [complexity]. Want me to send it over?"
"Wanted to follow up" "The HOA rules shifted in [community] — heads up before you go deeper on listings."
"Touching base" "I keep a running file of [school zoning / flood zones / STR rules]. Emailing it now."
"Circling back" "Another client in [market] just closed — the numbers might change your timeline."
Hard Rule
If your script does not contain a specific thing the lead can only get through you, it is a sales-pressure script. Rewrite it before you press dial.
