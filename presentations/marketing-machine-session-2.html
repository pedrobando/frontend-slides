Lead Magnet

What Is a Lead Magnet?

A specific, valuable piece of content your avatar receives in exchange for their information — that delivers immediately on the promise your hook made.

THE RULE: The lead magnet must solve the EXACT pain your hook promised to solve. Nothing more. Nothing less.

Option A

The Guide

“7 Steps From Renting to Owning in Kissimmee”

Option B

The List

“12 Homes Under $350K Available Right Now”

Option C

The Checklist

“What You Need to Get Approved (Even with Imperfect Credit)”

Option D

The Report

“Your Personal Payment Breakdown: How Much Can You Afford?”
