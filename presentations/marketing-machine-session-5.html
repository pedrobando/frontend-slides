Campaign Objectives

Meta Needs to Know What You Want

The objective trains the algorithm to find the type of person most likely to do that action.

OBJECTIVE 01
Leads
When you want people to submit their contact information.
OBJECTIVE 02
Engagement
When you want comments, messages, or interaction.
OBJECTIVE 03
Traffic
When you want people to visit a landing page.
OBJECTIVE 04
Awareness
When you want reach and visibility.
Don't pick an objective because it sounds good. Pick it based on the action you actually want.

How much did it cost to capture one lead?
METRIC 02

METRIC 03
Speed to lead
How fast did someone follow up?
METRIC 04
Response rate
How many leads replied after follow-up?
METRIC 05
Appointment rate
How many leads booked a consultation?
METRIC 06
Conversion rate
How many became clients?

The Follow-Up System

The Follow-Up Converts the Lead

Most Meta leads aren't ready to sign today. They need a real human cadence.

First 5 minutes
Text + Call + Email
Triple-touch. Speed is the single biggest lever.
First 24 hours
Send resource + ask one question
Deliver what they came for. Then open the conversation with one easy question.
First 7 days
Helpful info tied to their goal
Market context, related listings, education — not sales pressure.
First 30 days
Nurture campaign
Market education, new listings, invitations to talk. Stay top-of-mind.

Sample first text

Hey, this is [Name]. I saw you requested the buyer guide. I'm sending it over now.

Are you looking more seriously right now, or just starting to explore?

A lead is not a client. A conversation is the bridge.
