## Module 01 — The Psychology Shift / El Cambio de Mentalidad

### THE BIG IDEA — READ THIS TWICE

Most agents lose the lead in the first ten seconds of contact. Not because the lead is bad. Because the agent puts the sales pressure on the lead instead of on themselves.

### The Default Move (and why it fails)

> "Hi Maria, this is [Agent] at [Brokerage]. You submitted your info on my website, so I thought I'd give you a quick call and see if you have any questions."

**What happens in Maria's brain:**
1. She knows you are in sales.
2. She knows the only beneficiary of the call is you.
3. She defaults to the cheapest defense she has: "I am just looking."

That is not a Maria problem. That is a script problem. The pressure is on Maria to either commit or escape. She will always pick escape.

### The Move That Wins

Flip the pressure onto yourself. You go first. You provide. You volunteer something specific and valuable that the lead can only get through you. The lead is no longer being asked to commit. They are being asked to receive.

**FRAME TEST:** If a stranger handed you the voicemail you just left, would they say it sounds like sales or like service? If sales, rewrite it. Every time.

### Why Real Estate Is Different (and harder)

Tow trucks, LASIK surgeons, and emergency plumbers all start every conversation with built-in authority. The customer knows they need a professional and cannot get the outcome without one.

Real estate does not have that gift. The customer believes:
- Agents are overpaid.
- I can see the photos and the price online. I have all the information.
- If I ignore this agent, nothing bad happens to me.

### Add Complexity to the Sale

Complexity equals value. Strip complexity and you become a door-opener. Add complexity and you become a guide. Examples:

- **Central Florida:** HOA rules, CDD fees, and short-term-rental restrictions in Reunion, Champions Gate, Solara, and Storey Lake.
- **Kissimmee / Tampa:** Flood zones, insurance shifts, and roof-age underwriting changes.
- **Milwaukee:** Lake-effect property differences and waterfront tax implications.
- **Miami:** Investor-friendly markets in Brickell, Coral Gables, and Coconut Grove versus owner-occupied corridors.
- **Cross-Border:** Bilingual closing process for buyers between Florida, Wisconsin, and Puerto Rico.

**HARD RULE:** Never run a sales-pressure callback again. If you catch yourself saying "just checking in," "see if you have any questions," or "wanted to follow up," hang up before they answer and rewrite the script.

## Module 02 — The 5-Pillar Frame / El Marco de los 5 Pilares

### YOUR OPENING FOR EVERY CALLBACK, VOICEMAIL, TEXT, AND EMAIL

Every outbound contact you make to a new or active lead must cover these five pillars in order. No exceptions.

| # | Pillar | What it sounds like |
|---|--------|-------------------|
| 1 | Here's who I am | Name, brokerage, and the fact that you have already met through their website. |
| 2 | Here's why I'm calling | A specific, benefit-led reason. Never "just checking in." |
| 3 | I know what you're looking for | Mention the keyword, neighborhood, price range, or feature they searched. |
| 4 | I can help you get it | Reference our process, a current client, or a reThought service. |
| 5 | Here's a specific thing you can get through me | An offer. A market report. A neighborhood brief. Something they cannot easily get on Zillow. |

### The Keyword Is the Desire

Every lead that comes into Lofty came in through a specific search. That search is the desire. Honor it.

| Keyword | Desire |
|---------|--------|
| Lake Nona homes for sale | Master-planned community with schools and medical campus |
| Champions Gate pool homes | Short-term rental investment property |
| Casas en venta Kissimmee | Spanish-speaking buyer in our core market |
| Brickell condos with city view | Luxury urban Miami buyer |
| Brookfield homes for sale | Suburban Milwaukee family buyer |
| Doctor Phillips schools | Family relocating for school zoning |

### Script A — Central Florida Resort / STR Buyer

**KEYWORD: CHAMPIONS GATE POOL HOMES**

**EN:** Marco, this is [Agent] at reThought Real Estate. We met through my website, you were checking out some pool homes in Champions Gate. Heads up before you go too deep on listings, the HOA rules around short-term rental have shifted in a few of those communities and the CDD fees are not what most sites show. I just put together a one-page brief for another client running the exact same play. Want me to email you a copy today? No pressure, just useful.

**ES:** Marco, soy [Agent] de reThought Real Estate. Nos conectamos por mi sitio web, andas buscando casas con piscina en Champions Gate. Antes de que sigas viendo listings, te aviso, las reglas de HOA para renta corta cambiaron en algunas de esas comunidades y los CDD fees no son los que aparecen en los sitios. Ya armé un brief de una página para otro cliente que anda en lo mismo. ¿Te lo mando hoy por email? Sin presión, solo información.

### Script B — Family Relocating to Orlando Area

**KEYWORD: DOCTOR PHILLIPS SCHOOLS / LAKE NONA HOMES FOR SALE**

**ES:** Jennifer, soy [Agent] de reThought. Nos conectamos por el sitio, estabas viendo casas en el área de Lake Nona y Doctor Phillips. Mi equipo se especializa en familias que se mudan al área de Central Florida, y mantengo un archivo con los cambios de zonificación escolar, los tiempos de manejo, y la expansión del medical campus. Te lo mando ahora por email. Échale un ojo. Si algo te llama la atención, me escribes y armamos un tour virtual con nuestro proceso de relocation concierge.

### Script C — Milwaukee Suburb Buyer

**KEYWORD: BROOKFIELD HOMES FOR SALE / WAUWATOSA**

The reason I'm calling, I just walked through three properties in that corridor last week and the price-to-condition spread is wider than the search results show. Some homes are listed strong but need work, others are priced just below the comps but in great shape. I put a quick breakdown together for the buyer side of our team. Want me to send it over so you have it before you keep searching?

La razón de mi llamada, recorrí tres propiedades en ese corredor la semana pasada y el spread entre precio y condición es más amplio de lo que muestran los resultados. Algunas casas están bien listadas pero necesitan trabajo, otras están bajo los comps pero en buen estado. Le armé un quick breakdown a mi equipo. ¿Te lo paso para que lo tengas antes de seguir buscando?

### Script D — Bilingual or PR Cross-Border Buyer

**KEYWORD: CASAS EN VENTA ORLANDO / MUDANZA DESDE PUERTO RICO**

Nosotros trabajamos con muchos clientes que se mudan desde Puerto Rico a Central Florida, así que tenemos un proceso completo, desde encontrar la zona correcta para tu familia hasta coordinar con tu abogado de cierre en la isla. Te estoy mandando ahora nuestro brief bilingüe de relocation. Échale un vistazo y me dices si quieres montar un Zoom esta semana.

**CUSTOMIZE THIS WEEK:** Take all four scripts and rewrite them with three names of communities you actually serve, three real complexity points you know cold, and one current client story you can reference.

## Module 03 — The 3-Step Lofty Setup SOP / El SOP de 3 Pasos en Lofty

**REQUIRED FOR EVERY NEW LEAD, EVERY TIME, WITHIN 24 HOURS OF INTAKE**

| Step | Action | Outcome |
|------|--------|---------|
| 01 | Rename the search | Every email the lead receives has the keyword, your name, and your phone in the subject line. |
| 02 | Expand if narrow | Lead gets fresh inventory on theme, never goes silent for more than a week. |
| 03 | Schedule market report | Monthly inbound presence tied to their geography. Long-tail nurture happens automatically. |

### Step 1 — Spot the Keyword. Rename the Search.

Where the keyword lives in Lofty:
1. Open the lead's profile in Lofty.
2. Scroll to the source / activity timeline.
3. Find the first inbound action. The UTM content field carries the keyword.
4. If organic and keyword is missing, use the landing page URL as proxy.

**Rename format:** `[KEYWORD] by [Your Name] at reThought · [Your Phone]`

Example: "Champions Gate Pool Homes by Maria Lopez at reThought · 407-555-0142"

### Step 2 — Expand the Search If the Niche Is Too Narrow

How to know it's too narrow:
- Single subdivision with fewer than 10 active listings.
- Single zip code with very specific feature.
- Brand-new construction community where inventory is builder-controlled.

Expansion examples:
- Single community in Champions Gate → all STR-friendly resort communities in Polk and Osceola counties.
- Waterfront on a specific Milwaukee lake → comparable waterfront on adjacent lakes within 20 minutes.
- New construction in Lake Nona → all new construction within five miles.
- Single Brickell building → comparable luxury high-rises in Brickell and Edgewater.

### Step 3 — Schedule the Market Report

- Set geography to match the renamed search keyword. Do not default to the whole MLS.
- Set frequency to monthly. Upgrade to weekly only if the lead has shown urgency.

**The 24-Hour Rule:** Every new Lofty lead gets Steps 1, 2, and 3 completed within 24 hours of intake. This is the floor.

**HARD RULE:** If you do not complete Steps 1, 2, and 3 within 24 hours of a new lead, that lead is in arrears. The broker will see it on the scoreboard.

## Module 04 — The Hidden Decision Maker / El Decisor Oculto

### THE SINGLE BIGGEST GAP IN MOST AGENTS' FOLLOW-UP

More than 80% of buyers and sellers have a spouse, partner, or co-decision-maker. Lofty captures one name, one phone, one email. The other person is invisible.

That invisible person is the one who:
- Gets the recommendation from a friend to use a different agent.
- Sees a Zillow ad and signs up on a competing site.
- Has the deciding opinion on the school district, the commute, or the loan terms.
- Tells the lead in your CRM to stop responding to you.

### The Ask — How to Surface the Second Person

**EN:** Jennifer, one thing that makes a home search smoother is having both decision-makers on the same email and alert system. That way you do not have to forward listings back and forth or worry about missing a showing because one of you saw it first. Want me to add your spouse or partner so you both see what's coming through?

**ES:** Jennifer, una cosa que hace más fácil la búsqueda de casa es tener a las dos personas que deciden en el mismo sistema de alertas. Así no tienes que andar reenviando listings o preocuparte por perderte una visita porque uno lo vio primero. ¿Quieres que añada a tu esposo o pareja para que ambos vean lo que sale?

### If They Resist — The Soft Re-Ask

**EN:** Totally up to you. Just so you know, our team also offers a financing audit and a market analysis that some clients like to share with their spouse before they commit. If that becomes useful, just send their email when you're ready. No pressure.

**ES:** Sin problema, tú decides. Solo para que sepas, nuestro equipo también ofrece un análisis de financiamiento y un análisis de mercado que algunos clientes les gusta compartir con su pareja antes de comprometerse. Si en algún momento te sirve, me mandas su email cuando estés listo. Sin presión.

### Lofty Workflow
1. Open Relationships or Co-buyer / Spouse.
2. Add the second person with name, email, and phone.
3. Enable automated emails and alerts for secondary contact.
4. Tag the record with `HiddenDM-Captured`.

**SCOREBOARD METRIC:** Hidden Decision Maker capture rate. Industry average: below 20%. Our target: 60% of active leads inside 90 days.

## Module 05 — The Callback Architecture / La Arquitectura de Llamadas

### VOICEMAIL + EMAIL COMBO

80-95% of outbound calls land in voicemail. Plan your scripts for voicemail first, conversation second.

### The Two-Move Combo
1. Leave a voicemail using the 5-Pillar Frame, pointing the lead toward an email.
2. Send the email within five minutes. The email substantiates your authority frame.

### The Voicemail Formula (under 45 seconds)

| # | Move | Sample phrase |
|---|------|--------------|
| 1 | Name + brokerage + already met | "Hi Marco, this is [Agent] at reThought Real Estate. We met through my website..." |
| 2 | Acknowledge the keyword | "...you were checking out some pool homes in Champions Gate..." |
| 3 | Plant the complexity | "...heads up, the short-term-rental rules changed in a few of those communities and the CDD fees are not what most sites show." |
| 4 | Reference the email | "I just emailed you a one-page brief I put together for another client doing the same play." |
| 5 | Soft close, no pressure | "Take a look. If anything jumps out, text me back. Either way I'll keep sending the good ones your way." |

### Email Template — English

**Subject:** Champions Gate pool homes · short-term-rental brief inside

Marco,

Quick note from the voicemail. Attached is the one-page brief on short-term-rental rules and CDD fees for the Champions Gate area. The numbers in there are what we use to underwrite a property before we make an offer for a client.

If you want me to run a quick analysis on a specific property, just send me the address and I will turn it around the same day.

[Agent] | reThought Real Estate | [phone] | bilingual buyer support

P.S. If your spouse or partner is also in the search, send me their email and I will keep them in the loop too.

### Email Template — Español

**Subject:** Casas con piscina en Champions Gate · brief de renta corta adentro

Marco,

Como te dije en el voicemail, adjunto el brief de una página sobre las reglas de renta corta y los CDD fees en Champions Gate. Estos son los números que usamos para evaluar una propiedad antes de hacer una oferta para un cliente.

Si quieres que te corra un análisis rápido de una propiedad específica, me mandas la dirección y te lo devuelvo el mismo día.

[Agent] | reThought Real Estate | [phone] | soporte bilingüe para compradores

P.D. Si tu pareja también anda en la búsqueda, mándame su email y la mantengo al tanto también.

### The Three Outcomes

1. **They reply** — Move to qualifying questions. Schedule a Zoom, phone call, or showing.
2. **They open but don't reply** — Send second-touch email 48 hours later. New value, different angle.
3. **Silence** — Move into long-term nurture. They are 6-18 months out, not gone. The market report keeps you in their inbox.

**COUNTERINTUITIVE TRUTH:** There is no correlation between cost per lead and ROI. Conversion is a function of follow-up discipline, not lead quality.

## Module 06 — Response Triggers in Lofty / Disparadores de Respuesta en Lofty

### Default Setting: Tier 2

| Tier | Triggers | Why |
|------|----------|-----|
| Tier 1 (Mandatory) | New lead, contact form, showing request | People raising their hand. Missing these = presence problem. |
| Tier 2 (Mid-range) | Favorited a property, shared a property, returned to site after 7+ days, incoming text/chat | Behavioral signals. Leads moving inside the funnel. |
| Tier 3 (Intense) | Every available trigger, viewed any page, opened any email | Full visibility. Only if you have capacity. |

### The "Favorited a Property" Script

**EN:** Jennifer, this is [Agent] at reThought. We just got your showing request for [property address], but my system glitched and did not capture the time you wanted to come see it. Can you text me back with what works? If that time was a misclick, no problem, but I wanted to make sure I did not miss you.

**ES:** Jennifer, soy [Agent] de reThought. Acabamos de recibir tu solicitud de visita para [property address], pero mi sistema falló y no agarró la hora que querías ir. ¿Me escribes con la hora que te funciona? Si fue un click sin querer, no hay problema, pero quería asegurarme de no perderte.

**WHY THIS WORKS:** The favorite is a high-intent signal, but you cannot pitch it as "I see you favorited." That feels like surveillance. Framing it as a system glitch puts you in service mode.

## Module 07 — Post-Closing Sequence / Secuencia Post-Cierre

### The 4-Step Post-Closing Sequence

| Step | Action | Why |
|------|--------|-----|
| 01 | Take them to dinner within 30 days | Replace the closing-table memory with a relaxed dinner. Pay for everything. Do not talk shop. |
| 02 | Send pre-framed testimonial 2-3 days after dinner | Clients are not copywriters. You write the draft, they edit. |
| 03 | Post testimonial across every channel | Facebook, Instagram, Google, Zillow, website. One testimonial, ten placements. |
| 04 | Direct mail the testimonial to CRM + target geo | Postcard with closed property photo + testimonial + specific offer. 100-200 surrounding homes. |

### The Dinner Rules
- Within 30 days of close. Sooner if you can.
- You pay for everything. Dinner, drinks, Uber both ways, babysitter if needed.
- Do not talk real estate at the table. The dinner is human, not transactional.

### Pre-Framed Testimonial Template

**EN:** Hi [Client], it was great catching up at dinner last week. I am keeping a file of client experiences so future clients can hear directly from people we have worked with. Thinking back on our transaction, I jotted down what I remember about the value we delivered. Would you mind taking a quick read and editing it however you see fit?

**Sample draft:** "[Agent] from reThought helped us relocate from [origin city] to Central Florida and made it feel less like a transaction and more like having a guide. We had specific questions about school zoning in the Lake Nona area and concerns about closing while we still had to sell our home in [origin]. [Agent] handled the timing on both sides, connected us with a listing agent we could trust, and walked us through the bilingual closing process. We closed without having to move twice."

A single first transaction produces:
1. Seller-side post-close sequence (testimonial + direct mail wave).
2. Buyer-side post-close sequence (story-based mailer to past clients and geo farm).
3. Just-listed and just-sold display ad rotation across Google, Microsoft, and Meta.
4. Relocation referral campaign to agents in other markets.
5. Neighborhood farm mailer every 60 days.
6. Dinner-fueled organic referral 6-18 months later.

### Channel Discipline

| Annual Commission | Max Channels |

## Module 09 — Memorability and Forced Consumption

### Three Kinds of Memory
1. **Episodic** — something specific that happened to you.
2. **Semantic** — general knowledge you carry about the world.
3. **Procedural** — a sequence of steps you do without thinking.

Most agent marketing only triggers semantic memory. We engineer episodic memory into every piece of marketing.

### Story Beats (use real photos, not stock)
- You and client family at welcome dinner.
- Walking through a property on video with the buyer.
- Milwaukee waterfront lake at dawn — helping a Florida buyer relocate north.
- At the closing table with the bilingual closing attorney.

### Force Consumption
- Total number of reviews displayed in every email signature and landing page.
- Case studies sent to every active lead within first 7 days.
- Relocation concierge document for every relocation lead.
- Bilingual closing process document for every Spanish-language lead.
- Investor/STR brief for every resort-community lead.

### The Seller Pitch Page — Questions It Must Answer
1. How long have you been doing this?
2. What is your list-to-sale price ratio?
3. What is your average days on market?
4. Have wealthier or more experienced clients trusted you?
5. What do you do that other agents don't?
6. How long will the listing process take?
7. Can I just sell to an iBuyer?
8. Why use a boutique brokerage versus a big-box brand?
9. How much do you charge?
10. What is your marketing plan, specifically?
11. What happens if my house does not sell in 30 days?
12. Can you sell my house in another language to reach more buyers?
